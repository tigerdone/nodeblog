MagicPAPER Group come to ACM SIGCHI DIS 2018!

2018-06-13

The 6th ACM Symposium on Spatial User Interaction (SUI 2018) take place from October 13 to14 in Berlin, Germany. We have published a demonstration paper entitled " MagicPAPER: An Integrated Shadow-Art Hardware Device Enabling Touch Interaction on Kraft paper " and reported the research results in the field of HCI in the conference.

What is worth mentioning is that during the Demo-tracking time in 13th,October, many attendees showed high praises to our program, not only about the system, but also about the UI design etc. So the program has been invited to show in the poster-tracking time again in 14th , October, such a good way to show more people about our program, so amazing!
