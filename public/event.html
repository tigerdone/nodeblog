UIST Student Innovation Contest

2018-08-04

The Anmoji-planning has been accepted to the 2018 UIST Student Innovation Contest. During the next two month, we will develop the system by using the own device and the make-block that provided by the UIST sponsor. And the most exciting, as the only team in China, we will demonstrate our anmoji system in the conference and compete with top100 universities around the world in Berlin 9-13 October in 2018.Such a wonderful chance!
