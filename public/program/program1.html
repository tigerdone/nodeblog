The Legend of ‘Shi-Bi’

Tone of Qiang is an everyday language learning app which designed based on the Qiang corpus. Through the collection and collation of the existing Qiang language materials, the first complete slang corpus at home and abroad was established, and the Qiang language learning application was designed and developed on this basis.And it uses pictures, tests and situational exercises to learn Qiang language. In addition, we make picture books of the popular historical stories of the qiang people to let users know more about the culture of Qiang. This application can be applied in many fields such as education, tourism, cultural communication, etc., and has great migration value.
