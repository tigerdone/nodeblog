See your voice

The development of contemporary new media art is prosperous, but few researche rs develop new media art devices centering on the weak hearing impairment of tee nagers. In order to give the contemporary weak hearing impaired users an interesti ng new media art installation experience, we designed and implemented this soun d visual interactive art device for targeted hearing impaired users: See your voice. T he core of this device is to take various sound input and touch as interactive media, and to take visual interaction interface as real- time feedback, so that sound can trigger the user's heart in other sensory forms, an d feel the charm of rhythm, timbre and so on. At the same time, the public can bett er care for the disabled through this project, so that the society can help the hearin g impairment of teenagers.
