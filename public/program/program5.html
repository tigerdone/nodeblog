Qiang

Qiang, as the earliest ethnic mankind recorded in China, it is one of the most important orgins of the ancient Shu civilization.

Just relying on a white wall, we created such a new media installation by combining hand-made stickers, light and shadow art and magnetic card together. At the same time, we skillfully integrated the traditional Qiang nationality items to our interactions, such as the sheep totem, the sheepskin drum, the Yunyun shoes , the white stone and the pipa, and restored the Geji war scene in the small theater based on the daily life, national beliefs and history and culture of the Qiang people. So that people can feel the unique wisdom and charm of the ancient Qiang. Finally, We hope to attract the attention of the contemporary people. And all of us could pick up the magnetic card hardware, experience the simple and pure light and shadow art and the beautiful life of the Qiang people and try to inherit the Chinese traditional Qiang culture more.
