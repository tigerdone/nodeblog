The MagicPAPER Group won “the Single award of interaction design” in Life Geek 2018

2018-12-1

Facing the development and transition of technologies and art, some try to break through and create possibility to diversify; some explore artifact and nature by using comprehensive machinery and remote interaction; some break through traditional image creation and mode of dissemination to express their very unique perspective.

LIFE GEEK is a competition of art and technology, also a cultural journey, which give the young artists a platform to show. After 3 months of intense competition, the MagicPAPER group was shortlisted in the top20 and invited to shanghai to attend the opening ceremony of the display in December,1st.

What amazing is that as the world top20 group, this program have won the single award of interaction design with ￥1000. Congratulations to them.
