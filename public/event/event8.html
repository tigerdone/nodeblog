The MagicPAPER group come to ISS 2018

2018-11-29

MagicPAPER group has come to the 2018 ACM International Conference on ISS (Interactive Surfaces and Spaces), which take place from November 25 to 28 in Tokyo, Japan. ACM ISS — growing out of the successful conference series ACM ITS — is now in its third year, and is quickly emerging as the premier venue for sharing research on topics such as large-scale interactive displays, 3D and spatial user interfaces, touch/gesture/whole-body interaction, tabletop computing, media facades, interactive architecture, virtual and augmented reality, and many others that lie within the overarching theme of interactive surfaces and spaces.

In the Demo track, the project has won many attendees’ praises. The most exciting is that they have won the “Best demo honorable mention award”, Congratulations to them.
